Privacy Policy


The protection and security of your data is one of our main priorities. We want to inform you about the data you provide when visiting the site www.demo.school.inilabs.xyz, and how we collect, process, and use it.


We collect, process, and use the personal data of visitors to our site to improve the usability, efficiency, and security of our website, thereby providing visitors with the best experience.


We also collect, process, and use the information you send through contact or application forms or buttons to process inquiries or business inquiries, evaluate applications, register visitors for events, training sessions, or contests, and for other purposes you may specify.


Our website and the underlying technologies are subject to constant change and development. As a result, we may need to update this Privacy Policy from time to time to comply with global data regulations.


School Express reserves the right to modify or amend these terms and conditions at any time. Any changes will be posted on this website. You should periodically review this page to ensure you accept the latest terms presented.


Purpose of Data Collection


We collect, process, and store your data to process your requests. Whether you are interested in a vacancy at School Express, need software services from us, or want to provide services to us, we need to store your personal data to process your request.


We collect and process data automatically (via various add-ons in JavaScript code) every time you visit our website and both automatically and manually whenever you provide data through our contact forms. The data you send through contact forms is processed manually.


1. Your Information That We Can Collect, Process, and Use


School Express does not collect any personal data (personally identifiable information) without your permission and does not require such information to access and use this website.


Personal data includes all information relating to an identified or identifiable website visitor. When you provide data through this website or directly by email or telephone, we collect, process, and use only the personal data you submit and the necessary information to communicate with you about your inquiries or requests.


If you contact us through the contact form, you agree to allow School Express to collect and process the following data:


Your full name, email address, and phone number

Your affiliation – the name of the organization you are part of

The reason for your request


When you submit a request through the contact form, you also consent to allow School Express representatives to contact you by email or telephone. If you consent to share the data you have provided with third parties, School Express partners, or suppliers, this information will be shared accordingly.


If you apply for a position through our website, you consent to allow School Express to collect, process, and use the following data:

Your name, email address, and phone number

Your tech stack of interest

A message that you send voluntarily and knowingly

Your CV


When you apply for a position, you also consent to allow School Express representatives to contact you by email or telephone.


2. Use of Cookies and Web Server Data


As you browse the website, some non-personal information may be passively collected. School Express may collect information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system, and browser type.


Cookies are small text files placed on your computer by websites you visit. We use temporary (session) cookies and permanent (persistent) cookies. Session cookies are deleted when you leave, while persistent cookies remain unless you delete them.


If you do not want School Express to collect your non-personal information via cookies, you can configure your browser to reject cookies or notify you when one is sent.


www.demo.school.inilabs.xyz uses Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics, and tracking pixels from Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn campaigns. Cookies collected via these tools are used for internal reporting and to improve the website. These cookies collect information anonymously, including the number of visitors, where they came from, and the pages they visited.


For technical reasons, internet browsers automatically transmit some data to our web server when you visit the website. This data, stored by us or third parties, includes but is not limited to:


Your IP address

The date and time of your visit

Types of websites you visit

The URL of the page you visited previously

Information about your device and browser


This information is aggregated and analyzed anonymously to improve the user experience. Technical and organizational measures are in place to ensure compliance with data protection rules.


Data Collected


School Express limits the personal data it processes to information that personally identifies you (name, address, phone number, email address). If your inquiry is related to recruiting, we may process information about your education, work experience, and skills to match you with suitable positions. For business-related inquiries, we may record information about your business.


Transfer of Data to Third Parties


All details collected via the website are emailed only to the relevant department of School Express. The data you provide through contact forms or email is shared with third parties only if you consent. By submitting forms or inquiries, you agree that we may transfer this information to third parties for commercial offers or promotional purposes related to your request.
